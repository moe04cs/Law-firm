Strategic Legal Disciplines

Our firm operates at the intersection of law and business strategy. Below are our five core practice areas, each led by dedicated specialists committed to protecting your assets and fueling your growth.

Corporate & Business Contracts

Building a business requires a solid legal foundation. We provide end-to-end corporate support, drafting contracts that not only protect you today but scale with your growth.

  • Company Formation: Comprehensive registration for LLCs, Corporations, and Non-profits, including bylaw creation and operating agreements.
  • Mergers and Acquisitions: Full-service buy-side and sell-side support, from initial due diligence to final closing and post-merger integration.
  • Shareholder Agreements: Defining clear ownership rights, voting protocols, and exit strategies to prevent future internal disputes.
  • Business Contracts: Drafting airtight vendor, supplier, and client agreements that minimize liability and clarify deliverables.

Conflict Resolution & Employment

Disputes can drain resources and damage reputation. We prioritize efficient resolution through mediation while safeguarding your workforce management.

  • Arbitration & Mediation: Utilizing alternative dispute resolution methods to settle conflicts privately and cost-effectively, avoiding lengthy trials.
  • Debt Recovery: Professional negotiation strategies and legal demand letters to recover outstanding assets while maintaining business relationships.
  • Employment Law: Creating compliant employee handbooks, non-compete agreements, and defending against wrongful termination claims.
  • Enforcement: Navigating the legal system to execute local and international court judgments and secure what is owed to you.

Real Estate & Finance

Whether you are acquiring commercial property or securing capital for expansion, our team navigates the complexities of property law and financial regulations.

  • Real Estate Transactions: Drafting and reviewing commercial leases, purchase agreements, and handling title transfer procedures.
  • Finance Agreements: Structuring secured loans, convertible notes, and private equity investment contracts to fuel business growth.
  • International Services: Assisting foreign investors with local property acquisition, Foreign Direct Investment (FDI) laws, and visa requirements.
